Neurosexism: A Conversation

With Elisa Carrasco Lanusse, Lissie Morales, and Evan Stowe

As the final project for "Knowledge and Society: Sex, Gender, Sexuality, and the Brain Sciences", we decided to create a podcast episode as a way of more accessibly engaging with the content of the course. We particularly focus on how forms of injustice are reproduced through scientific study. By interrogating some of the studies we talked about in the course, we hope to problematize Western academia's perspective and suggest the need for inclusion beyond the subject of study and behind the research itself.

Thank you to Jolie for the help with the recording and to everyone in our class for providing your insights, knowledge, and experiences and creating a safe space to learn!
