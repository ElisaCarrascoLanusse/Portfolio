Labor Violations: Anti-Trafficking In Persons In Eastern Missouri

As an intern with the International Institute of St. Louis' ATIP team, I researched labor violations in Eastern Missouri, analyzed data to identify patterns, and created infographics and reports to support stakeholder engagement and client resources.

  • Role: Research, data analysis, and design
  • Tools: R Studio, Procreate, and Figma
  • Duration: 7 months

Memory Matters: Comparative Analysis of Memorialization

As a researcher on Memory Matters, I collaborated with Dr. Quinsaat and a team to develop an analytical framework for memorial sites in Bosnia, Cambodia, and Rwanda. I focused on the Kigali Genocide Memorial, contributing to site analysis and the broader framework.

  • Role: Research and writing
  • Tools: Zotero and Canva
  • Duration: 4 months

Laboratoria: Unlocking Engagement

As an intern at Laboratoria, I worked closely with the partnerships team to improve the design of newsletters and foster deeper bonds with students, graduates, and stakeholders. I conducted UX research and design to enhance the communication strategy.

  • Role: Research, design, and user testing
  • Tools: Figma, Procreate, Hubspot, R Studio
  • Duration: 6 months
